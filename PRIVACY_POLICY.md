# Privacy Policy — WA1500 Timer (PPC Timer)

**Effective date:** 2026-02-03

## Summary
WA1500 Timer is a Range Officer timer app. The app is designed to work offline and does not require an account.

## Data collection
The app does **not** intentionally collect, sell, or share personal information.

## Network usage
The app may use the local network (Wi‑Fi) to discover and control external signal horn devices using UDP.

The app does not require cloud accounts and does not include third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs.

## Permissions
Depending on Android version and device behavior, the app may request:

- **Internet / Network access**: for optional local network features.
- **Wi‑Fi state / multicast**: to discover devices on the local network.
- **Location (coarse/fine)**: some Android versions require location permission for Wi‑Fi scanning and device discovery.

Location permission is used only to enable Wi‑Fi discovery features on supported Android devices. The app does not use location permission to track you.

## File access
The app may allow selecting audio files (if you enable custom sounds). Selected files remain on your device.

## Children’s privacy
This app is intended for use by adults (Range Officers) during sport competitions.

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL.
